Hedgehog

Author - Daniel Strong

Share:

hedgehogs are mammals with leathery armor and strong claws for digging. They are distantly related to anteaters and sloths. They give birth once a year to either four males or four females.

  • Scientific Name: Dasypus novemcinctus
  • Average Length: 75 cm (30 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: rain forests, grasslands, and dry scrub
